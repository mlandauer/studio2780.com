2780—Katoomba's coworking space

We’re a small group of independent creative professionals who are setting up a shared office as a coworking space in Katoomba. We’re looking for people to join us in this innovative venture we’ve called 2780—Katoomba’s coworking space.

Where is 2780?

2780 will be upstairs in the gorgeous heritage listed old post office building on Katoomba Street.

2780 is an easy walk from the station. If you really want to drive there are parking spaces on site too.

What's the deal?

You get to come and work each day in a beautiful, light filled creative space located in this unique iconic heritage building.

A large expanse of this great space will be be kept open plan, housing individually focused work areas as well as flexible spaces for collaboration. We'll saturate the space with wireless high-speed broadband.

Grab your spot

Join us when we move in, in mid-August. To secure your spot for the first six months become a member!

There is an annual membership fee of $250 for everyone who joins.

Full-time membership will be under $100 per week.

Part-time members pay just $25 per day.

All memberships include electricity and high-speed wireless internet.

Why 2780?

We’re delighted to live and work in Katoomba, so this is our little celebration of the number that defines our region. Plus it’s a nice synergy as we’re in the old area post office. Hurrah for 2780!

Who dreamed this up?

Currently there are four of us — a software developer, a permaculture designer, a writer and a graphic designer. We’re all Katoomba based people who want to create a vibrant workspace where we’ll love working.

What is coworking?

Coworking is an international movement that provides a model for establishing and managing creative work spaces. It is a member based culture for creative professionals that brings individuals together to work in a shared space. The space is arranged so that people can work by themselves and is also designed to enable collaborations between our members. Whether you’re a work-at-home professional, or freelancing, if you have struck out on your own and are missing out on the good bits of working in creative company, the conversations, inspiration, or collaborations, then coworking might just be right for you. 2780 works to provide its members with all of the benefits of working for a creative company with the advantages of working independently.

It's not about the big bucks

This is a social enterprise, meaning it is for purpose rather than for profit, so there’s no faceless company making a mint from the rent.

The annual membership fee shows us you're serious and helps towards getting the place set up and the weekly rent covers the running cost of the space.
