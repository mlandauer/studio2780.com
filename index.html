2780—Katoomba's coworking space

Update: We've gone into hiatus. Our initial six month experiment has come to an end.

What does this mean?

We took over the upstairs space at the gorgeous heritage listed old post office building on Katoomba Street for a period of six months.

We don't have that space anymore — there's a bunch of reasons that happened.

What's happening now?

An unincorporated association "Katoomba coworking" still exists and through that members may hold events to promote coworking and collaboration. Stay tuned.

Interested in being part of the next space?

Please email us at hello@studio2780.com so that we can add you to our list of people to contact when the next thing gets off the ground.

What is coworking?

Coworking is an international movement that provides a model for establishing and managing creative work spaces. It is a member based culture for creative professionals that brings individuals together to work in a shared space.
